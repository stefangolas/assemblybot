These screws are immune to mild vibration and fit for assemblies that require smooth, accurate movement. A threadlocker patch is bonded to their threads, which saves you the hassle of adding it yourself. The patch keeps the screw from coming loose. These screws are engineered for tight tolerances, with shoulder diameters twice as precise as those on standard-fit screws.

18-8 Stainless Steel—The choice for wet and outdoor environments, these screws won't rust. However, they won't hold up to the harsh chemicals that 316 stainless steel can.
